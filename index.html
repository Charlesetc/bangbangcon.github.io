!!Con (pronounced bang bang con) is two days of ten-minute talks about what excites us about programming — the amazing, the strange, and the heartwarming. From kernel exploits to teaching kids to program, !!Con is about bringing the NYC programming community together to commemorate the excitement, joy, and surprise in what we do.

The conference will be held at Hacker School, near the intersection of Broadway and Grand Street in Manhattan. Tickets to !!Con do not cost money, but space is limited.

Registration isn't open yet, but we're accepting talk proposals now. We'd love to hear yours.
