Accessibility information

Important: !!Con has moved to a NEW LOCATION in Brooklyn, NY!

!!Con 2015 will be held at the NYU MAGNET space, located at 2 MetroTech Roadway, 8th Floor, Brooklyn NY 11201. We'll update this page as soon as possible with accessibility details about our new space.

We will have real-time captioning in English of all the talks at !!Con.

!!Con is fun, but hectic. During the entire event, we will have designated quiet rooms for people who want to take a break from the excitement.

Do you require other accommodations or have concerns or questions about accessibility at !!Con? We're here to help! Contact the organizers.

Thanks to AlterConf for their help with this page.
