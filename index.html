!!CON 2016

The joy, excitement, and surprise of programming

May 7-8, 2016 in NYC

!!Con (pronounced "bang bang con") 2016 is two days of ten-minute talks (with lots of breaks, of course!) to celebrate the joyous, exciting, and surprising moments in programming.
