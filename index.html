!!Con 2015

The joy, excitement, and surprise of programming · May 16-17, 2015 in NYC

!!Con (pronounced bang bang con) is two days of ten-minute talks (with lots of breaks, of course!) about what excites us about programming — the amazing, the strange, and the heartwarming. From kernel exploits to teaching kids to program, !!Con is about bringing the NYC programming community together to celebrate the joy and excitement and the surprising moments in what we do. We define "programming community" broadly — you don't need to be a professional programmer to be part of !!Con.
